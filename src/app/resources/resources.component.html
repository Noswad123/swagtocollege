College Resources 
  1. College Flyins
    This link provides a list of colleges that offer fly-in programs. Fly-in programs are programs where colleges fly seniors to visit their campus. If the student is accepted for the fly-in during their senior year, the college will pay for the senior to come and visit.
  2. Full-Need Colleges
    This link provides a list of colleges that are full-need schools. Full-need schools are colleges that will pay all of a student's demonstrated financial need and thus are more affordable for low-income students. 
  3. College Research
    This link provides a list of questions that may help a student choose a college.
  4. Essay Tips
    This link provides tips for writing the college application essay.
  5. College Financial Information
    This link provides  information about work study, student loans, scholarships, the CSS profile, and FAFSA that may be new to first-generation college applicants.
  6. Tips on Handling College Application Results
    This link provides information about how to deal with college acceptances/rejections as well as tips on how to choose the right college.
  7. Reminders for College Deadlines
    This link provides tips and reminders for the different college deadlines to stay on top of (this includes financial documents, medical forms, etc).

Parent Resources 

  1. Parent/Guardian Letter
    This letter provides parents/guardians with information about the SWAG To College program. The letter also contains the contact information for the SWAG To College Co-Founders, whom you contact with any questions.​
  2. Padre/Tutor Carta 
    Esta carta provee a los padres / tutores con información sobre el programa de SWAG Para la Universidad. La carta también contiene la información de contacto para el botín a la SWAG Para la Universidad Co-Fundadores, a quienes se ponga en contacto con cualquier pregunta.
  3. Parent FAFSA Flyer
    This flyer highlights important deadlines and information about the FAFSA. Reading this will help you and your family understand the basics of the FAFSA.
  4. Padre FAFSA Folleto 
    Este folleto destaca los plazos importantes y la información sobre la FAFSA. Leer esto le ayudará a usted ya su familia a entender los fundamentos de la FAFSA.
  5. Parent FAFSA Details
    This document details the FAFSA and explains each step parents need to take in order to help their children navigate and complete the FAFSA. As compared to the flyer, this document is much more detailed and comprehensive.  
  6. Padre FAFSA Detalles
    Este documento detalla la FAFSA y explica cada paso que los padres deben tomar para ayudar a sus hijos a navegar y completar la FAFSA. En comparación con el folleto, este documento es mucho más detallado y completo.
  7. SAT/ACT Explainer
    This documents provides parents/guardians with information about the SAT and ACT and which test students should take and how they can prepare for it.
  8. SAT/ACT Explicador
    Este documento proporciona a los padres / tutores información sobre el SAT y el ACT y qué pruebas deben tomar los estudiantes y cómo pueden prepararse para él.
  9. Scholarships and Grants
    This document provides parents/guardians with information on how their child can secure scholarships and grants to help pay for college.
  10. Becas y subvenciones
    Este documento proporciona a los padres o tutores información sobre cómo su hijo puede obtener becas y subsidios para ayudar a pagar la universidad.

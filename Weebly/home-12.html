STUDENTS WITH AMBITION GO TO COLLEGE
Our vision is to pair youth with mentors from similar backgrounds. We pair high school students with college mentors who help them navigate the college process. We also pair college students with Young Professional mentors who support students transition from college to the workforce. ​

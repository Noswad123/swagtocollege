Katia Ventura
Director of Social Media
I am a rising senior at Washington & Jefferson College, majoring in Psychology and minoring in Latin American Studies. I am the SWAG To College Social Media Director, and Social Media Chair for the Black Student Union at Washington & Jefferson College. I love being a part of SWAG To College because I am able to use the power of social media to enact social change inside the education sector with the main goal of providing help to high school students in order for them to reach their goal of going to college. After college I would like to pursue a JD or an MBA inside of the marketing and advertising sector while doing volunteer work for organizations who aim to create equal education opportunities
